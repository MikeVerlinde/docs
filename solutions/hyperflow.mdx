HyperFlow transforms AI capabilities into automated, event-driven workflows. Build sophisticated processes that trigger AI actions automatically, chain multiple agents together, and respond intelligently to real-time events.

## Key Features

- **Event-Driven Architecture**: Automatically trigger AI workflows based on any event
- **Visual Builder**: Design complex workflows with our intuitive drag-and-drop interface
- **Intelligent Routing**: Dynamic decision making for complex process flows
- **Built-in Monitoring**: Real-time visibility into your AI workflows
- **Enterprise Ready**: Scale from simple automations to complex, multi-stage processes
