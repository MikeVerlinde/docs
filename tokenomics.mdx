HyperMinds is a utility token for the HyperMinds ecosystem, designed to provide access to the platform and its features. HyperMinds launched via PumpFun, meaning 100% of the supply was added to the liquidity pool (80% via bonding curve, 20% directly).

## Risks
- The HyperMinds token is tradeable on decentralized exchanges, which means its value can be volatile and subject to market fluctuations.
- No future value of the token is guaranteed. The token may lose its value in part or in full.
- The token may not always be transferable.
- The token may not be liquid.
- Trading cryptocurrency carries inherent risks, and you should never invest more than you can afford to lose
- Smart contract risks exist despite security audits and best practices
- Regulatory changes in different jurisdictions may impact the token's utility and tradability
- The token's utility is limited to the HyperMinds ecosystem features and services
